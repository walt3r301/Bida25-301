Skin Problems

Acne

Acne is a common skin condition that occurs when hair follicles become clogged with oil and dead skin cells. It can cause pimples, blackheads, and whiteheads.

Oily Skin

Oily skin happens when the sebaceous glands produce too much oil (sebum). This can make the skin look shiny and may lead to acne.

Dry Skin

Dry skin occurs when the skin lacks moisture. It may feel rough, tight, or flaky and can be caused by weather, dehydration, or harsh products.
